An empathetic companion for people living with dementia — and the caregivers who love them.

Yadira gives families a gentle, voice-first space for reassurance, memory, and connection. It is built to validate feelings, reduce distress, and support daily caregiving — without replacing human care.

  • Validation over correction
  • Comfort over compliance
  • Caregiver partnership
  • Dignity first

Why Yadira exists

Dementia often creates a painful mismatch between what a person feels and what the world keeps insisting is “real.” Traditional software often responds with correction. Yadira responds with companionship.

The approach is inspired by validation therapy (Naomi Feil): meet the person where they are, acknowledge the emotional truth, and gently redirect only when helpful. In practice, this means fewer confrontations, less panic, and more moments of calm connection.

Who it's for

  • People living with dementia who need reassurance and familiarity
  • Family caregivers managing emotional load and daily routines
  • Professional care teams supporting many residents at once

What it is not

  • Not a medical device
  • Not a diagnosis or treatment tool
  • Not a substitute for clinicians or emergency services

“Reality orientation can increase anxiety in some moments. Validation can restore trust.”

How Yadira works in daily life

1) Build a familiar voice

Caregivers add letters, photos, and voice notes to shape a persona the loved one recognizes.

2) Offer a calm channel

Hands-free call mode and gentle sensory rooms (Aurora/Beach) create a low-friction way to self-soothe.

3) Preserve continuity

Session Memory helps the companion carry context from one conversation to the next.

Companion behaviors

  • Validates emotions (“That sounds scary; I’m here with you.”)
  • Avoids blunt contradiction when it would escalate distress
  • Uses short, grounding prompts and familiar routines
  • Supports redirection to food, hydration, rest, and safe activities

Caregiver controls

  • Routines and check-ins
  • Conversation context inputs
  • Optional AI-generated care reports

Safety, boundaries, and transparency

Important boundaries

  • Yadira does not diagnose or treat disease
  • In emergencies, contact local emergency services
  • Care plans remain clinician-directed

When to escalate to humans

  • Sudden severe confusion or behavior change
  • Falls, injuries, chest pain, breathing distress
  • Any situation suggesting immediate risk
Data use philosophy: data is used to run and improve the service for families, not sold for advertising profiles.
