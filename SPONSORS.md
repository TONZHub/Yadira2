# Sponsor Yadira

**Yadira is free for families.** A daughter should never have to reach for a
credit card to hear her mother's voice again.

We keep that promise the only honest way it can be kept: the people and
institutions who *can* pay — care facilities, foundations, and benefactors who
believe no one should grieve without a container — sponsor the families who
can't. That's the whole model. Families get the companion; institutions
sustain it.

> The reborn doll costs thousands and cannot speak. The mechanical cat costs
> $160 and cannot remember your wife's name. Yadira costs pennies a
> conversation and can be Beth. Accessibility isn't a feature here — it's the
> entire point. Sponsorship is how we protect it.

---

## For care facilities & memory-care units

This is Yadira's real home. If you run a memory-care unit, assisted-living
community, or hospice program, a partnership gives your residents the
companion **and** gives your staff the tooling built around it:

- **Every resident, no per-family paywall** — natural loved-one voices,
  hands-free Call Mode, calming sensory rooms, and Session Memory that carries
  each resident's world from visit to visit.
- **Caregiver & clinical tooling** — the redirection cues that translate a
  nurse's note into something a resident can hear ("I'm in the kitchen
  starting your eggs, come find me"), AI daily-routine plans, and the clinical
  insight tracker (sleep/hydration/mood trends, sundowning patterns) across
  your residents.
- **Validation-therapy aligned** — built on Naomi Feil's principle of meeting
  a resident where they are, never correcting them. It fits how your best
  staff already work.
- **Onboarding support** — we help your team build each resident's persona from
  the family's letters, photos, and voice notes.

Pricing is per-unit, not per-family, and we scale it to the size of your
program. **The families in your care never see a charge.**

**→ Start a partnership:** email `partnerships@yadira.app` *(placeholder —
replace with your real contact)* with your facility name, resident count, and
a good time to talk. We'll walk you through a pilot on one unit.

## For individual benefactors

Not a facility, but this matters to you? The **Sponsor button** on the repo
(and GitHub Sponsors, once enrolled) lets you keep the companion running for
families directly:

- **$5/month** — sustains one family's companion, indefinitely.
- **$25/month** — a small cluster of families who couldn't otherwise afford it.
- **Custom / in memory of someone** — reach out; we'll tell you exactly how
  far it goes, and (with your permission) who it helped.

Every dollar is spent keeping Yadira free for a family, not on ads or growth
hacks. We'd rather have ten families who'd be heartbroken if we shut it off
than ten thousand who never opened it twice.

---

## Transparency

We'll publish, in plain numbers, how many families are sustained and what it
costs to run them (the AI conversations, the natural voices, the hosting).
Sponsoring grief demands a higher bar for honesty than most software — and if
you ever suspect a number here is dressed up, tell us, because that would
betray the entire reason this exists.
