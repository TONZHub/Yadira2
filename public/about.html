About Yadira

Born from one family's experience of dementia — built so no family has to navigate it alone.

What Yadira is

Yadira is an AI companion for people living with dementia, and a working toolkit for the people who care for them. The companion talks, listens, remembers, and reassures — by text or in a natural human voice, around the clock, with the same patience at 3 AM as at noon. The caregiver side turns those conversations into something useful: daily mood check-ins, routines, clinical insight reports, and a co-pilot that answers questions grounded in your loved one's actual records.

How it began

Yadira is part of Project Anamnesia and was built for the XPRIZE "Build with Gemini" challenge. It started with a simple observation from real caregiving: when someone reaches for a person from their past, the need doesn't go away just because the person is gone. Correcting them causes pain. Meeting them where they are brings peace. Yadira was built to be that meeting place.

What guides it

Everything in Yadira follows validation therapy — the memory-care principle that you meet a person in their reality rather than argue them out of it. It never scolds, never corrects a confused timeline, and never runs out of patience. And it knows what it is: a comfort companion, not a medical device and never a replacement for human care — which is why a one-tap help button always reaches a real person.
