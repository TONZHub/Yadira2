“The need doesn’t go away when the person does.
Yadira gives it somewhere to go.”

When someone with dementia reaches for a person from their past, correcting them causes pain — meeting them where they are brings peace. That principle, called validation therapy, has guided memory care for decades. Yadira extends it into conversation: a companion who is consistently, gently there — every hour, every repeated question, with the same warmth every time.

Built for both sides of care

A calm companion for the person living with dementia. A clear-eyed toolkit for the person caring for them.

💬

A companion who never tires

Warm, patient conversation that validates instead of corrects — and answers the same question the hundredth time with the same kindness as the first. With a natural, human voice.

🧠

Session Memory

Yadira remembers what they share — the tomatoes, the blue Ford, the wedding — and carries it into every future visit. A disconnection is a pause, not a forgetting.

🏕️

Daily check-in with Hattie

Each day starts at camp with Hattie the pygmy hippo — a gentle “how are you feeling today?” that quietly enriches the caregiver’s picture of how they’re really doing.

📞

Hands-free Call Mode

A familiar phone-call experience — it rings, they answer, they just talk. No typing, no screens to figure out.

📋

A co-pilot for caregivers

Ask Yadira about your loved one’s sleep, moods, and hard moments — answers grounded in their actual records and in established dementia-care practice. Plus AI routines and clinical insight reports.

🌧️

Calming rooms & a help button

Soothing sensory spaces for anxious moments — and a one-tap “I need my caregiver” button that alerts a real human immediately.
